How to Play ToeTacTic
1
Objective: Unlike traditional Tic-tac-toe, your goal is to LOSE by creating a line of three of your own symbols.
2
Taking Turns: Players take turns placing their symbols (❌, ⭕, etc.) in empty cells on the grid.
3
Winning/Losing: If you create a line of three of your symbols (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally), you lose the game!
4
Strategy: Try to force your opponent to create their own line while avoiding creating your own.
